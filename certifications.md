## Free certificates

### 5. Deep Reinforcement Learning Course
- **Provider:** HuggingFace
- **Covers:** Phase 04 — Reinforcement Learning
- **Cost:** Free
- **Certificate:** Yes (complete 80% of assignments, models evaluated on HuggingFace Hub)
- **Link:** https://huggingface.co/learn/deep-rl-course
- **What you get:** 8 units covering Q-learning, DQN, policy gradients, Actor-Critic, PPO, multi-agent RL. Practical assignments using Stable-Baselines3.
- **Why it matters:** Your trained models get published to HuggingFace Hub — public proof of work

## Recommended order

1. Start Phase 00-01 (Python, C++, Math) while watching free YouTube content
2. Enroll in **Machine Learning Specialization** during Phase 02
3. Enroll in **Deep Learning Specialization** during Phase 03
4. Get **PyTorch Certificate** alongside Phase 03
5. Do **HuggingFace Deep RL Course** during Phase 04 (free, great hands-on practice)
6. Do **HuggingFace Robotics Course** during Phase 05 (free, directly relevant to VT course)
7. Phases 07-08 are your actual VT course — no separate certification needed

## Financial aid on Coursera

Every paid Coursera course has a financial aid option:
1. Click "Enroll" on any course
2. Click "Financial aid available" 
3. Fill out a short application (takes 10 minutes)
4. Wait 15 days for approval
5. Get full access for free including the certificate

Approval rate is very high. This is a legitimate program, not a workaround.
